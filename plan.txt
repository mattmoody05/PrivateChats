Commands
    - private new
    - private @person
    - private close

new
    - gives them them the room role
        - the role will then give them access to the text chat and the voice chat
    - send dm saying that they have been given the role and they can now join

@person
    - gives the person mentioned the correct role
    - sends dm

close
    - takes away role
    - sends a dm to all the people saying that their role has been taken away
